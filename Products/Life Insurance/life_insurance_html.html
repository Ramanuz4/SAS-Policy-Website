What is Life Insurance?

Life insurance is a contract between you and an insurance company where you pay regular premiums, and in return, the insurer provides a lump sum payment (death benefit) to your beneficiaries when you pass away. It's a crucial financial tool that ensures your family's financial security and helps maintain their standard of living even in your absence.
